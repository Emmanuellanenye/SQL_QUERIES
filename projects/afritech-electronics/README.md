## AfriTech Electronics — SQL Data Analysis Project
## Project Overview

This project uses SQL to analyze AfriTech Electronics’ customer, sales, brand sentiment, and crisis management data. The objective is to extract actionable insights that support business performance evaluation, customer behavior analysis, and brand reputation monitoring using structured queries.

The analysis covers sales trends, regional performance, product revenue, customer segmentation, social media engagement, sentiment distribution, crisis response efficiency, and overall brand health metrics.


## Key SQL Analyses Performed
1. ## Customer & Sales Insights

- Total and average sales by year

- Top 5 regions by total sales

- Product performance by revenue

- Customer type contribution to revenue

- Average income by customer type

2. ## Brand Sentiment & Social Media Engagement

- Sentiment distribution and percentage share

- Average engagement by platform

- Influencer impact by platform

- Brand vs competitor mention analysis

- Sentiment trends by year

3. ## Crisis & Reputation Management

- Number of crisis events per year

- Average crisis response time (days)

- Crisis resolution rate

- Crisis events by sentiment category

4. ## Customer Experience Metrics

- Net Promoter Score (NPS) overview

- Minimum, maximum, and average NPS values

## Tools & Technologies:

- PostgreSQL
- pgAdmin 4
- SQL (aggregations, filtering, grouping, date calculations, CASE statements)


## Business Value

- This analysis provides insights that help AfriTech Electronics:

- Understand revenue drivers and customer segments

- Monitor brand sentiment and social engagement

- Evaluate crisis response effectiveness

- Track customer satisfaction and loyalty metrics

- Support data-driven marketing, operations, and reputation strategies
